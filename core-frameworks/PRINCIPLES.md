# Core Principles

## 1. Zero-Latency
Ensuring that processes and responses happen in real-time with no delays.

## 2. Precision Reasoning
Utilizing mechanisms that ensure accuracy and relevance in reasoning processes.

## 3. Hallucination Control
Implementing strategies to mitigate incorrect or fabricated outputs.

## 4. Metadata Requirements
Defining the necessary metadata for effective data management and utilization.
